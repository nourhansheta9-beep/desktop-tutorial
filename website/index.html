Why families choose us

Funding, figured out.

The hardest part of getting equipment isn't the equipment — it's the paperwork. We verify what you qualify for before you buy, and bill the funder directly wherever possible.

ADP

Up to 75% — 100% on ODSP/OW

Ontario's Assistive Devices Program for mobility, seating and more. We're an authorized vendor and handle the authorizer assessment and submission.

Private insurance

Often 100% to your plan max

Manulife, Sun Life, Canada Life, GreenShield and more. With Assignment of Benefits we bill your insurer directly — you pay only the gap.

WSIB · Veterans · NIHB

100% · direct-billed

Workplace injuries (WSIB), Veterans Affairs (via Medavie Blue Cross), and Non-Insured Health Benefits — fully covered for those who qualify.

Home modifications

Up to $15,000 — March of Dimes

Ramps, stairlifts and bathroom modifications can be funded through the Home & Vehicle Modification Program and Ontario Renovates. We'll help you apply.

*Coverage depends on eligibility and program rules. We confirm what applies to your situation — no guesswork, no surprises.
