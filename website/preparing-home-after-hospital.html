Preparing your home before coming back from hospital

4 min read · Home safety

A safe homecoming starts before discharge day. Use this checklist to get the home — and the right equipment — ready in time.

Room by room

  • Bedroom: consider a hospital bed or bed rails; clear a path to the door
  • Bathroom: grab bars, a raised toilet seat, and a shower chair or transfer bench
  • Living areas: remove loose rugs and clutter; add a lift chair if standing is hard
  • Entry: a ramp or threshold ramp if there are steps

Get equipment in place early

Equipment is often needed the same day someone comes home. Renting is a fast, affordable way to have a bed, wheelchair or lift ready before discharge — and we can deliver and set up within 24–48 hours (same-day for urgent discharges).
