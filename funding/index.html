Programs we help you access

We match you to the right program and complete the application.

Assistive Devices Program (ADP)

ADP

Who it’s for: Ontario residents with a valid health card and a long-term (6+ month) physical disability.

What it covers: Up to 75% of the cost of approved mobility & seating devices — manual and power wheelchairs, scooters, walkers and positioning/seating.

How to apply: An assessment by a registered ADP authorizer (often an OT or PT) confirms your device. We help arrange it and complete the paperwork.

Ontario Disability Support Program (ODSP)

ODSP

Who it’s for: People receiving the Ontario Disability Support Program.

What it covers: Health-related and disability benefits that can help fund equipment such as homecare beds, lifts and bathroom-safety items.

How to apply: Coverage is through mandatory and discretionary benefits — we help you request the right support.

Ontario Works

OW

Who it’s for: People receiving Ontario Works.

What it covers: Discretionary benefits that may cover some assistive devices and home-safety equipment.

How to apply: We help you ask your caseworker for assistive-device coverage and provide the quotes they need.

WSIB

WSIB

Who it’s for: People with an approved WSIB (workplace-injury) claim.

What it covers: Health-care equipment and assistive devices related to your claim.

How to apply: Share your claim details and we coordinate approved equipment directly with WSIB.

March of Dimes — Home & Vehicle Modification (MOD)

MOD

Who it’s for: Eligible Ontarians who need home or vehicle accessibility changes.

What it covers: Grants toward ramps, stairlifts, porch & platform lifts, ceiling lifts and bathroom modifications.

How to apply: We help you apply and provide the assessments and quotes the program requires.

Spinal Cord Injury Ontario

SCIO

Who it’s for: People living with a spinal cord injury or a similar disability.

What it covers: Equipment-access programs plus guidance to other funding you may qualify for.

How to apply: We help connect you to the right program and prepare what's needed.

Eligibility and coverage are set by each program and depend on your situation — we’ll help you confirm what you qualify for.

Questions? Answers.

The things people ask us most, before they get in touch.

Do I have to pay full price for my equipment?
Often, no. We help you access funding programs like ADP, ODSP, Ontario Works, WSIB, March of Dimes and Spinal Cord Injury Ontario, which can cover part — or all — of the cost. Ask us what you may qualify for.
Can I rent instead of buying?
Yes. Hospital beds, wheelchairs, scooters, stairlifts, walkers, lift chairs and patient lifts are available to rent by the month — ideal for recovery or short-term needs.
Do you deliver and install?
Yes. We deliver across the Greater Toronto Area and install equipment such as stairlifts, ramps and lifts so it’s ready to use safely.
Do you repair and service equipment?
We do — 7 days a week, with same-day repairs across the GTA and loaner equipment available while yours is serviced.
Which areas do you serve?
We serve the Greater Toronto Area.
How do I get started?
