## What has to be true (this is capacity-gated, not magic)

These numbers assume you make the moves in the other docs — the levers don't fire without the people to run them:

1. **Funding & Intake desk hired (1–2)** — without it, insurance/WSIB/VAC/NIHB/HVMP paperwork doesn't scale and approvals leak. *This is the gate on ~$45k/mo of the Base lift.*
2. **2nd technician / cross-trained delivery** — recurring PM + rental growth needs service capacity beyond Abdelrahman alone.
3. **Referral / Community Liaison hired** — feeds the funnel the uplift assumes.
4. **HVMP process** — someone owns helping clients apply for March-of-Dimes/Renovates on every accessibility quote. (Biggest single lever for *your* catalog.)
