## The 3 missing roles that actually unlock 2–3×

These don't exist yet and are the highest-leverage additions:

1. **Funding & Intake Desk (NEW) — the #1 hire.** Owns benefit triage for *every* lead across ADP/ODSP/insurance/WSIB/VAC/NIHB/MOD, the authorizer (OT/PT) roster, predeterminations, AOB billing, and denial management. In a funding-gated business this desk *is* the throughput multiplier — it converts the demand your sales team already generates into approved, paid units. **Hire 1–2.**
2. **Referral Development / Community Liaison (NEW).** Fills the top of the funnel that Nourhan's inbound line depends on: systematic relationships with hospital **discharge planners**, **OTs/PTs**, physicians, Ontario Health atHome care coordinators, and retirement-home wellness nurses. Ideally someone with a clinical or OTA background. **Hire 1.**
3. **Billing / RCM (NEW or carve-out).** Claims, AR, denials, predeterminations across all payers. Can start inside the Funding desk and split out as volume grows. **Hire 1 (or assign).**

## The 2 bottlenecks to fix immediately

- **Only one technician (Abdelrahman).** Service, preventive-maintenance contracts, and repairs are a recurring-revenue pillar — and a single tech is a hard ceiling. **Add 2 techs** and/or **cross-train 2 of the delivery team** for basic setup/PM/repairs. Make Abdelrahman the **Service/Technical lead**.
- **Double-hatting splits focus.** Mohamed Hassany runs both reactive service inquiries *and* proactive service-sales (different motions). **Moayed** is split across B2B *and* outside sales. Pick one primary lane for each (see below) and back-fill the other.

## What changes for each person

- **Nourhan** — stays inbound, but now armed with verified-funding scripts (no more "let me check if it's covered"). Gets a real CRM and lead-source tracking so we know which referral relationships pay off.
- **Mohamed Alyoussef** — elevated to **Institutional BD lead**: hospitals/Hospital-at-Home, ORCA + AdvantAge memberships, Ontario Health atHome prequalification/formulary. This is the "be the #1 hospital vendor" owner.
- **Moayed** — moved fully to **Outside/Field acquisition** with Mamoun and Mohamed Teskie (stop the B2B split).
- **Mamoun Alnajdawy** — **Field acquisition lead**; target ADP/ODSP/MOD clients and feed the Funding desk.
- **Mohamed Teskie** — field acquisition; pair with the Community Liaison on referral territories.
- **Mohamed Hassany** — moves to **Service-Sales / Account Management lead** (the recurring-revenue engine). His old reactive "service inquiries" queue is handled by an Operations service coordinator (can be one of the new hires or Noura's team) so he can sell, not just answer.
- **Nabil** — Purchasing now also owns **inventory + rental-fleet stock planning** (tight loop with Operations).
- **Delivery team (5)** — become "**delivery + light service/PM + resupply eyes-and-ears**"; 2 cross-trained for setup/repair; all trained to flag reorder/upsell signals on every visit.
- **Abdelrahman** — **Service/Technical lead**; builds the PM-contract program and trains the new/cross-trained techs.
- **Noura** — Operations now owns **dispatch/scheduling, rental asset tracking, and the service-coordinator queue**; partners with Nabil on inventory.
- **Saeed** — owns the institutional contracts (Ontario Health atHome, hospital SLAs), the funding-registration roadmap, and the KPI dashboard.
