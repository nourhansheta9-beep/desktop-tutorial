# 07 · Operations · Purchasing · Delivery & Logistics

**Lead / owner:** **Noura**  •  **Mandate:** make Help Mobility the **fastest, most reliable** fulfillment in the GTA — because "speed + setup" is how we beat the national chains and win Hospital-at-Home. Operations turns approved orders into delighted, on-time installs and keeps the rental fleet and inventory humming.

## Team & roles
- **Noura — Operations Manager.** Owns dispatch/scheduling, delivery routing, the **rental-fleet asset tracking**, inventory planning (with Nabil), and the **service-coordinator queue** (the reactive "service inquiry" intake moved here so Service-Sales can sell). Owns on-time %, fulfillment SLA, and gross-margin discipline on logistics.
- **Nabil — Purchasing.** Owns supplier relationships, cost negotiation, inventory levels, and **rental-fleet stock planning**. Targets: stock availability, COGS reduction, and turns. Sources refurbished/trade-in stock for the resale line.
- **Delivery & Logistics — Ahmed Dakak, Moatasem, Sami, Rashid, Raef.** Deliver, set up, and orient on every order; run rental delivery/pickup/clean/redeploy; **2 of them cross-trained for basic setup/PM** to relieve the single-technician ceiling. Every driver is trained to flag **reorder/upsell/safety** signals on each in-home visit (paid spiff).

## Income lines owned / enabled
- **Delivery / setup / installation fees** (billable).
- Enables **rental** revenue (utilization depends on fast turn/clean/redeploy) — the Hospital-at-Home wedge.
- **Refurbished/trade-in resale** sourcing (Nabil) — margin-rich.
- Margin protection: COGS, route efficiency, asset utilization.

## Targets & KPIs
- **On-time delivery/setup %** (target ≥ 95%); fulfillment cycle time.
- **Rental fleet utilization %** and turn time (deliver→clean→redeploy).
- Inventory availability (no lost sale to stockout); **COGS / gross-margin %**; inventory turns.
- Driver **upsell/reorder flags** generated (feeds 04).
- Service-coordinator response time on inbound service inquiries.

## Playbook (how it runs)
1. **Daily huddle** with Delivery + Technical: today's routes, installs, rentals, urgent repairs, blockers.
2. **Rental as a managed asset:** every unit tracked (location, status, due date); fast clean/redeploy = more revenue from the same fleet.
3. **Route optimization** for same/next-day promises; same-day lane for acute Hospital-at-Home.
4. **Inventory discipline** (Nabil): par levels on fast movers; rental stock planning with Institutional's pipeline; negotiate COGS down.
5. **Drivers as sensors:** every visit, flag aged equipment, resupply-due, safety upgrades → Service-Sales.
6. **Refurb pipeline:** intake trade-ins/returns → refurbish → resell.

## Compensation
Ops/Purchasing: base + bonus on **on-time %, rental utilization, and gross-margin**. Delivery team: base + **spiffs for upsell/reorder flags that convert** and for PM/setup work (cross-trained). COGS-reduction bonus for Purchasing.

- ↔ **Service/Technical (08):** install/PM work orders, refurb.
