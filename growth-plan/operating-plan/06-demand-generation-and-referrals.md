# 06 · Demand Generation & Referrals  (NEW)

**Lead / owner:** **Community Liaison** (new hire — ideally clinical/OTA background)  •  **Mandate:** fill the top of the funnel that every other pod depends on. In this industry, demand is **prescriber- and OT-driven** — someone must own those relationships systematically instead of hoping leads show up.

## Team & roles
- **Referral Development / Community Liaison.** Owns relationships with **hospital discharge planners, OTs/PTs, physicians, Ontario Health atHome care coordinators, and retirement-home wellness nurses.** Runs lunch-and-learns and education sessions, keeps a referral-source CRM, and is the friendly, fast point of contact that makes Help Mobility the easy referral. Also owns basic **marketing**: web/SEO, Google Business, reviews, and the leave-behind/deck collateral.

## Income lines influenced (credited to the pods it feeds)
- Inbound retail leads (→ 01), field/accessibility/ADP leads (→ 02), institutional intros (→ 03).
- Online discovery → web-store + assisted sales (→ 01).
- Review/reputation flywheel → cheaper, warmer leads everywhere.

## Targets & KPIs (leading indicators for the whole company)
- **Active referral sources** (OTs/PTs/physicians/discharge planners/homes) — grow monthly.
- **Qualified leads generated by source** (proves ROI; ties to the +15% referral uplift in the model).
- Lunch-and-learns / education sessions held; new referral accounts opened.
- Google Business profile + **review volume/rating**; web lead volume.

## Playbook (how it runs)
1. **Referral target list** per territory (mirrors Field's territories) — hospitals, clinics, OT practices, retirement homes.
2. **Make referring effortless:** one number, fast response, "we handle the funding paperwork," the discharge **leave-behind** PDF in every hand.
3. **Educate:** quarterly lunch-and-learns on ADP/HVMP/insurance navigation — position Help Mobility as the funding experts.
4. **Track every source in CRM** so we double down on what converts (kill what doesn't).
5. **Reputation engine:** systematic review requests after good service (with 04); keep Google Business and the website current.
6. **Reactivate dormant referrers** the way Service-Sales reactivates customers.

## Compensation
Base + bonus on **active referral sources** and **qualified leads delivered that convert** (attributed via CRM source tagging). Quarterly bonus tied to the pods' referral-sourced revenue.

## Handoffs
- → **Inbound (01) / Field (02) / Institutional (03):** routes leads, tagged by source.
- ↔ **Funding Desk (05):** co-runs OT/physician education (funding is the hook).
- ↔ **Service-Sales (04):** review/reputation requests.

## 90-day priorities
1. Build the **referral-source CRM** and the territory target list.
2. Get the **leave-behind** into every local discharge unit and OT practice.
3. Run the first **lunch-and-learns**; open the first batch of referral accounts.
4. Stand up the **review/reputation** engine (Google Business + post-service asks).
