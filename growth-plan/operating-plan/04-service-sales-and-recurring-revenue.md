## Playbook (how it runs)
1. **Segment the database:** by equipment age, warranty status, ADP re-eligibility date, last resupply, rental end date. This list *is* the revenue.
2. **Cadenced outreach** (use `re-engagement-sequencing`, `customer-referrals`, `follow-up-discipline` skills): "your equipment is due / you're re-eligible / time to resupply."
3. **Launch resupply** as an opt-in subscription with auto-reminders and easy reorder.
4. **Sell PM contracts** to every institutional account (with 03) and to private owners of complex equipment.
5. **Capture repair billing:** every service visit gets coded and billed to the right funder where eligible (with Funding Desk + Technical).
6. **Referrals & reviews:** happy service customers are your cheapest growth — ask every time.

## Compensation
Base + **highest commission rates in the company on recurring** (resupply subscriptions, PM contracts, rental renewals) — because recurring compounds. Bonus on reactivation revenue and on lifting the recurring-revenue %.
