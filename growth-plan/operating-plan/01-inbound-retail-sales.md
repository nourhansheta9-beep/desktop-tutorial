# 01 · Inbound / Retail Sales

**Lead / owner:** **Nourhan**  •  **Mandate:** never let a qualified inbound lead die — convert calls, web orders, and walk-ins into funded sales, and maximize attach/upsell.

## Team & roles
- **Nourhan — Inbound Sales Lead.** Answers inbound calls, web-store orders, and walk-ins. Owns first-response speed, qualification, quoting, and conversion. Routes every lead through the funding triage (warm hand-off to the Funding Desk) so price is never the reason a deal dies. Logs every lead + source in the CRM.
- *(Add 1 inbound rep when call volume justifies — first scaling hire in this pod.)*

## Income lines owned
- Retail equipment sales (mobility, **bathroom safety & daily-living** — your highest walk-in/online categories), accessories.
- **E-commerce orders** (the site already has accounts/orders — turn it into a real channel).
- Accessory **attach** on every sale (cushions, batteries, ramps, trays) — pure margin.
- Hand-off of bigger-ticket/ADP/HVMP leads to Field (02) and accessibility jobs.

## Targets & KPIs
- **Dec monthly target:** $35–45k.
- Speed-to-lead **< 5 min** on calls / **< 1 hr** on web leads.
- Lead→quote conversion, quote→sale close rate, **% of quotes with verified funding**.
- **Accessory attach rate** (target every transaction has ≥1 add-on).
- Web-store revenue growth; abandoned-order follow-up rate.

## Playbook (how it runs)
1. **Every lead captured** in CRM with source (this is how Demand Gen proves ROI).
2. **Qualify fast** using the installed `discovery` / `qualifying-leads` / `asking-effective-questions` skills; capture clinical + funding info on the intake form.
3. **Triage funding before quoting** — "good news, this is likely covered" beats a raw price. Warm-transfer to Funding Desk.
4. **Quote same day;** follow a fixed follow-up cadence (`follow-up-discipline`, `email-sequence` skills) — most lost retail sales are just un-followed-up.
5. **Attach + upsell** with `cross-sell-upsell-detection`; offer financing on any gap.
6. **Web store:** chase abandoned carts, add "we handle your funding" messaging, capture phone for assisted sale.

## Compensation
Base + commission on **collected** revenue; **accelerator on accessory attach** and on converting web leads to assisted sales. Spiff for funding-verified quotes (drives the right behavior).

## Handoffs
- → **Funding Desk (05):** every order needing ADP/insurance/WSIB/VAC/NIHB.
- → **Field (02):** accessibility/HVMP and complex/high-ticket leads.
- → **Operations (07):** confirmed orders for scheduling/delivery.
- ← **Demand Gen (06):** inbound leads from referral campaigns (tagged by source).

## 90-day priorities
1. CRM + lead-source tagging live; speed-to-lead SLA enforced.
2. Funding-verified quoting becomes standard (no naked price quotes).
3. Web-store conversion push (abandoned-order follow-up, funding messaging).
4. Accessory-attach program with a simple "every order +1" goal.
