# 05 · Funding & Intake Desk + Billing / RCM  (NEW — the #1 hire)

**Lead / owner:** **Saeed** until a Funding Lead is hired  •  Staff: **1–2 Funding & Intake Coordinators + 1 Billing/RCM** (new)  •  **Mandate:** convert the demand the sales pods generate into **approved, paid** units — fast. In a funding-gated business, *this desk is the throughput multiplier.* It does not sell; it **unlocks** every revenue line for clients who otherwise couldn't pay.

## Team & roles
- **Funding & Intake Coordinator(s).** Run benefit triage on **every** lead (one intake form, every payer). Own the **authorizer roster** (OTs/PTs/physicians who assess/authorize), ADP submissions, insurance predeterminations + Assignment-of-Benefits, WSIB/VAC/NIHB packages, and March-of-Dimes HVMP application support. Track each file to approval.
- **Billing / RCM Specialist.** Direct-bills ADP/insurers/WSIB/VAC/NIHB, manages AR, works **denials** (log → fix → resubmit), and reconciles collections. Owns cash conversion.

## Income lines unlocked (credited to the sourcing pods, driven by this desk)
- **Insurance via Assignment of Benefits** (Manulife/Sun Life/Canada Life/GreenShield) — items often 100% up to $5–10k/yr; predetermination > ~$500.
- **WSIB, VAC (Medavie), NIHB** — 100%, direct-billed new payer pools.
- **ODSP-100% conversion** on existing ADP clients (75% → 100%).
- **HVMP/Renovates** application support for accessibility jobs.
- Faster, higher-rate **ADP** approvals across all categories.

## Targets & KPIs (this desk is measured on *throughput*, not a revenue line)
- **% of pipeline with verified funding** (target: ~100% before quoting).
- **Approval rate** and **approval cycle time** (drive cycle time down hard — it's cash *and* hospital trust).
- **$ unlocked / month** (revenue that exists only because the desk got it approved).
- **Denials recovered**; AR days; % direct-billed.
- Authorizer roster size & assessment turnaround.

## Playbook (how it runs)
1. **One intake form** triages every payer at first contact (template: `intake-funding-triage-form.docx`).
2. **Authorizer network** — a managed roster of OTs/PTs/physicians on call. *Approvals are usually gated by authorizer availability, not demand — so this network is the real constraint to manage.*
3. **Predetermination factory** — standardized templates (`insurance-predetermination-letter.docx`), submitted before delivery; track every one.
4. **HVMP desk** — help eligible accessibility clients apply (`hvmp-application-checklist.docx`).
5. **Denial management** — every denial logged with reason, fixed, resubmitted. Denials are recoverable revenue.
6. **Daily stand-up** — approvals due, predeterminations pending, denials to resubmit.

## Compensation
Base + bonus on **throughput**: approval rate, cycle-time reduction, $ unlocked, and denials recovered. (Pay the desk to *move volume*, not to file paper.)

## Handoffs
- ← **All revenue pods (01–04):** every funded deal arrives here with a complete intake.
- → **Operations (07):** approved orders released for scheduling/delivery.
- → **CEO:** new payer registrations + ADP category roadmap.

2. **Enroll** insurance AOB + WSIB + VAC + NIHB; run the **ODSP sweep**.
3. Build the **authorizer roster** (the real bottleneck) and the predetermination factory.
