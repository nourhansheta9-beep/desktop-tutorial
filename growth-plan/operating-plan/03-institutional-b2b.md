1. **Map the GTA institutions:** hospitals with Hospital-at-Home, discharge units, retirement/LTC chains. Prioritize a **beachhead** (2–3 hospitals + 1–2 chains).
2. **Win on speed + funding-done-for-you + reliability,** never inducements. Get on **approved lists/formularies**; protect patient choice (compliance slide is in the deck for a reason).
3. **Rental program** as the wedge into Hospital-at-Home (fast, low-friction, recurring).

5. **Associations:** join **ORCA** (commercial partner → 240+ partners' channel), **AdvantAge**, **OHMEPA** for access and intel.
6. **Quarterly business reviews** with each account (utilization, spend, outcomes) to retain and expand.
