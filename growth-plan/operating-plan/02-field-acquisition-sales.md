## Income lines owned (the high-ticket ones)
- **Home accessibility:** stairlifts, porchlifts, ramps, ceiling/floor lifts, door openers — funded via **March of Dimes HVMP ($15k home / $15k vehicle), Ontario Renovates, insurance, self-pay.** *This is the single most under-leveraged lever for your catalog — ADP doesn't fund these, so most shops never push them.*
- **ADP mobility & seating** (chairs, scooters, walkers) at 75–100%.
- **Complex rehab seating & positioning** — high ASP, ADP-funded (new focus).
- **Vehicle modifications** (HVMP).
- New-payer sourcing: identify **WSIB / VAC / NIHB** clients and feed the Funding Desk.

## Targets & KPIs
- **Dec monthly target:** $50–65k (largely net-new on top of the $100k base).
- **HVMP/accessibility jobs closed/month** (Base ≈ 5 → Stretch ≈ 12 @ ~$6k each).
- In-home assessments booked & completed; assessment→sale rate.
- Funded-deal cycle time (with Funding Desk); average approved value (ASP).
- New WSIB/VAC/NIHB clients sourced.

## Playbook (how it runs)
1. **Territory plan** across the GTA; each rep owns referral relationships in their patch (with Demand Gen).
2. **In-home assessment = the close.** Show up, measure, recommend; use `discovery`, `objection-handling`, `negotiation`, `sales-psychology` skills.
3. **HVMP-first reflex:** on every accessibility lead, screen HVMP/Renovates eligibility and **help the client apply** (use the HVMP checklist template). Make this a standard step, not an afterthought.
4. **Bundle:** pair a funded core item with self-pay accessories/financing.
5. **Hand every funded deal to the Funding Desk** with complete intake; keep selling while paperwork runs.
6. **OT alignment:** recommendations should match what an OT will authorize — fewer rejections, faster ADP approvals.

## Compensation
Base + **tiered commission on approved/collected** revenue. **Highest accelerator on HVMP/accessibility jobs and complex-rehab** (the high-ASP, under-leveraged lines). Bonus for sourcing a *new payer* client (first WSIB/VAC/NIHB deal each month). Team bonus on hitting the pod number.
