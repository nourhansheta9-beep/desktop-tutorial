# 08 · Service & Technical

**Lead / owner:** **Abdelrahman**  •  **Mandate:** turn service from a cost center into a **profit center** — billable repairs, preventive-maintenance contracts, install quality, and refurbished resale — while removing the single-technician bottleneck that otherwise caps growth.

> **Critical fix:** one technician (Abdelrahman) cannot support a 2–3× business. Add **2 technicians** and/or **cross-train 2 delivery staff** (with 07). Abdelrahman becomes the **lead** who builds the program and trains the team.

## Team & roles
- **Abdelrahman — Service/Technical Lead.** Owns repairs, **PM-contract delivery**, complex installs (stairlifts, ceiling/floor lifts, ramps), refurbishment quality, parts, and tech training/standards. Sets service SLAs.
- **Technician(s) (new / cross-trained).** Field repairs, PM visits, deliveries-with-setup, refurb work.

## Income lines owned
- **Billable repairs** — coded and billed to **ADP / insurance / WSIB / private** (stop giving away fixes; capture them). 
- **Preventive-maintenance (PM) contracts** — delivery arm of the recurring program (retirement/LTC fleets + private complex equipment).
- **Installation revenue** — accessibility installs (stairlifts/ramps/ceiling lifts) tied to HVMP/insurance jobs.
- **Refurbished / trade-in resale** — refurbish returns/trade-ins into resale stock (margin-rich).
- Warranty/"Mobility Care Plan" service delivery.

## Targets & KPIs
- **Repair-billing capture rate** (% of service visits invoiced to a funder/private vs given away).
- PM visits completed on schedule; **PM contract fleet size** serviced.
- First-time-fix rate; service SLA (response + resolution time).
- Refurb units produced & resold; install quality (rework rate).
- Technician utilization (and is capacity the constraint?).

## Playbook (how it runs)
1. **Bill every eligible repair** — with Funding Desk, code repairs to ADP/insurance/WSIB; private-pay the rest. (This is found money today.)
2. **PM program** — scheduled maintenance routes for institutional fleets + private complex equipment; documentation supports retirement/LTC **RHRA** compliance.
3. **Install excellence** — accessibility installs done right the first time (drives reviews + referrals).
4. **Refurb line** — intake trade-ins/off-rental units → refurbish → QA → resale (with Ops).
5. **Train & standardize** — Abdelrahman documents SOPs and trains the new/cross-trained techs (removes key-person risk).
6. **Feed Service-Sales** — every visit surfaces upgrade/replacement/resupply opportunities.

## Compensation
Base + bonus on **billable-repair capture, PM completion, and refurb output**. Quality gate: rework/callback rate must stay low (don't reward speed that creates returns). Spiff for upgrade/replacement leads passed to Service-Sales.

## Handoffs
- ← **Service-Sales (04):** PM + repair + warranty work orders.
- ← **Field (02) / Institutional (03):** accessibility installs + institutional PM.
- ↔ **Operations (07):** scheduling, parts, refurb, cross-trained delivery techs.
- ↔ **Funding Desk (05):** repair-billing eligibility/coding.

## 90-day priorities
1. **Add capacity** (2 techs / cross-train 2 delivery) — the #1 constraint.
2. **Turn on repair billing** — stop giving away service; code to funders.
3. **Launch PM contract delivery** (with 04 + 03).
4. **Start the refurb resale line** (with 07); document SOPs + train the team.
