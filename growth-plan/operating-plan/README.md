## The three moves that matter most

1. **Hire the Funding & Intake Desk (05)** — it unlocks ~$45k/mo of the Base lift by converting demand you already have into approved, paid units.
2. **Make HVMP screening mandatory on every accessibility quote (02)** — your stairlift/ramp/lift catalog is the highest-ticket, most under-leveraged lever, and ADP doesn't fund it.
3. **Build recurring revenue (04 + 08)** — resupply, PM contracts, billable repairs, rental renewals. One-time sales don't triple a company; recurring does.
